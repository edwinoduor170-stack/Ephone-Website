## Project Overview
* Product Catalog: A database of available phones with details like price, specs, and stock levels.
* Sales Management: Handling sales transactions, * payment processing, and order fulfillment.
* Inventory Management: Tracking stock levels, managing suppliers, and optimizing inventory.
* Customer Management: Storing customer data, managing interactions, and providing support.
* E-commerce Platform: An online storefront for customers to browse and purchase phones.
